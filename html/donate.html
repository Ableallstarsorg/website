We Need Your Help
The ball courts are funded 100% by your tax exempt contributions. Donations are accepted through this website specifically for the purpose of funding the construction of the ball court, audio and lighting for the ball court, equipment such as balls, nets, paddles, and staffing of Able AllStars Organized Group Play as well as Junior and Adult Pickleball Academies.

Bridging Communities & Sport

Every contribution to Able AllStars, in partnership with WallBall Pickleball, is a step towards enriching lives through sport. Your donations are pivotal in constructing a world where the Special Needs community thrives in unity with the exhilarating game of pickleball. When you choose to donate, you are directly supporting the creation and maintenance of our bespoke pickleball courts, designed inclusively to welcome players of all abilities. Your generosity helps us provide the latest equipment that makes the game accessible and enjoyable for everyone. It also enables us to run specialized programs that bring the joy of pickleball to children and adults alike, fostering a sense of belonging and accomplishment. Whether it's for casual family play sessions or structured training in our Junior and Adult academies, your donation is vital in keeping the spirit of the game alive and kicking. Invest in our mission to make pickleball more than just a sport, but a tool for growth, joy, and community connection. Every dollar you donate is an investment in a future where every member of our community gets an equal chance to serve, score, and soar. Together, we can ensure that pickleball remains a sport for all, growing and nurturing with every swing.
