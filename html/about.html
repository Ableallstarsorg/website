Bridging Communities & Sport

Able AllStars, in collaboration with WallBall Pickleball, offers a specialized training center uniting the Special Needs community with the rapidly growing pickleball sport. This synergy provides ideal spaces for family play and both Junior and Adult pickleball academies. We rely entirely on tax-exempt contributions to fund our courts, equipment, and programs. Every community can benefit from our venues, offering a haven for family fun and essential training for budding pickleball enthusiasts. Our uniquely designed courts cater to the special needs community while also serving as a top-notch training facility for America's fastest growing sport, pickleball.
